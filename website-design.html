Not Just a Website

A website is only useful if people can find it, use it, and trust it.

Every site I build is engineered around four things most templates and DIY builders quietly skip. These aren't add-ons — they're baked in from the first wireframe.

SEO

Search Engine Optimization

Clean semantic HTML, fast page loads, structured metadata, and content built around what your customers actually search for. Your site shows up when someone Googles your service in your town.

AEO

Answer Engine Optimization

People are asking ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews instead of scrolling search results. I structure your content so AI tools quote your site as the answer — not your competitor's.

Accessibility

Built for Everyone

Real contrast, real keyboard support, real screen reader compatibility. Accessible sites aren't just legally safer — they convert better, rank better, and welcome every customer through your digital front door.

Mobile Ready

Designed Thumb-First

Most local searches happen on a phone, often standing on the sidewalk. Your site is designed for that moment first — fast, readable, tappable — then scaled up to desktop, not the other way around.
