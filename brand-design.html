About

A design leader who still loves to make things.

I've spent the last decade at the intersection of design and engineering, leading teams while staying hands-on with the work. I've scaled a design org from 1 to 8, shipped products that grew user bases by tens of thousands, and built design systems from the ground up, often implementing them in code myself.

My path to design leadership started with earning my BFA in Graphic Design from Pacific Union College, where I focused on brand identity and interactive design. That foundation has shaped how I approach both visual systems and user experiences.

Today I'm most energized by building something meaningful: a product, a team, or a system that helps both thrive. I want to be in the work, not just directing it from the sidelines.
