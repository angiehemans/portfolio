The project

This was an arts and culture publication for a nonprofit, where the editorial side and the digital side needed to feel like one thing. The aim was a reading experience that did justice to the writing and artwork while being practical for a small team to publish and maintain.

Because it was a nonprofit effort, the project rewarded doing more with less: a clear visual system, a layout that put the content first, and a site that was straightforward to keep up to date.

My role

I led both design and development. I shaped the look and feel of the publication and then built the website that delivered it, owning the work from visual design through to implementation.
