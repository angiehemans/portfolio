About

I'm Angie — a design leader who still loves to make things.

Great design doesn't live in a vacuum. It lives in the code, in the systems, and in the way teams collaborate to ship.

I've spent the last decade at the intersection of design and engineering, leading teams while staying hands-on with the work. I've scaled a design org from 1 to 8, shipped products that grew user bases by tens of thousands, and built design systems from the ground up — often implementing them in code myself.

Today I'm most energized by the challenge of building something meaningful. Whether that's a product, a team, or a system that helps both thrive, I want to be in the work, not just directing it from the sidelines.
